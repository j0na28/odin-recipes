Orecchiette Pasta with Broccoli Sauce

Orecchiette pasta is combined with broccoli, toasted pine nuts, and Parmesan cheese, then broiled until crisp and golden. Great comfort food!

Amazing recipe just for you!

Ingredients:

  • Orecchiette pasta
  • Broccoli
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven's broiler. Lightly butter a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Add pasta, cook for 8 minutes, then stir in broccoli, and cook 3 minutes more.
  3. Broil in preheated oven until the cheese has crisped and turned golden brown, about 4 minutes.
